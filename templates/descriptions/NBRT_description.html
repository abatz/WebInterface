NBRT

The Normalized Burning Ratio Thermal Index (NBRT) uses a thermal band to enhance the NBR. It results in a better separability between burned and unburned land (Holden et al., 2005). NBRT1 was originally developed for use with Landsat TM and ETM+ bands 4, 7, and 6. However, it will work with any multispectral sensor (including Landsat 8) with bands that fall within the following ranges: NIR: 0.76 to 0.9 µm SWIR: 2.08 to 2.35 µm Thermal: 10.4 to 12.5 µm
