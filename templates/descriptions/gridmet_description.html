gridMET Gridded Observations
(Abatzoglou, 2013)

  • Surface meteorological dataset based on PRISM and NLDAS-2
  • Spatial resolution: 4-km grid (1/24-deg)
  • Time Span: 1979-Present(Updated every day)
  • Variables:
    • Minimum/Maximum Temperature
    • Precipitation
    • Minimum/Maximum Relative Humidity
    • Specific Humidity
    • Downward Solar Radiation
    • Near Surface Wind Velocity
    • Potential Evapotranspiration/li>
    • Energy Release Component(model-G)
